HANNAH & ROB

OUR STORY

We met seven years ago on September 12th, 2018, at Sneaky Dee's over nachos and immediately hit it off talking about dogs and making each other laugh - we were both so excited we called our friends that night to tell them we'd met someone special.

Since then, we've traveled everywhere from Mexico's temples to a random donkey sanctuary we discovered in the English countryside during our Isle of Wight trip. After three years together, we moved to Yonge & Eglinton, which felt right since Rob had lived there when we first started dating, and we love going back to our old spots like the comedy club and our weekend brunch place. Hannah finished her Master's and got into software development while Rob started his journey in veterinary medicine.

On our five-year anniversary, Rob proposed at Alexander Muir Memorial Garden - the same place where we first made things official - with our dog and close friends there. Two months later we got Boji, our incredibly affectionate puppy who loves everyone he meets but definitely destroyed our couch.

These days we spend time reading on our balcony, making each other laugh with Rob's dancing and Hannah's spontaneous singing, we argue about Survivor, play tennis together or against each other depending on our mood, cook whatever we feel like trying, and walk around the neighborhood with our very social dog.

We are thrilled to share this special moment in our lives with you. Join us as we embark on this new chapter, celebrating our union surrounded by the people who mean the most to us.
